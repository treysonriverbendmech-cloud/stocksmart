Features
Everything you need to
stay on top of your stock
📍
Per-Van & Per-Location Inventory
Every part is tracked by location — each tech's van, the shop, or a warehouse. See exactly what's where at a glance.
🔗
Housecall Pro Auto-Sync
Completed HCP jobs automatically queue inventory deductions for your review. Approve with one tap — no double entry, ever.
📷
Receipt Scanning
Snap a photo of any hardware store receipt and AI extracts the items and adds them to your inventory automatically.
🤖
AI Part Search & Matching
Voice search, smart fuzzy matching, and AI-powered suggestions mean you find the right part even when you don't spell it perfectly.
⚠️
Low Stock Alerts
Set minimum quantities for critical parts. Know before you run out — not after a tech is stranded on a job.
📊
Usage Reports
See what's moving, who's pulling it, and what needs restocking. Full inventory value, usage history, and reorder lists.
👥
Team Access Control
Techs access their own van. Admins see everything. Transfers are tracked. No more inventory blind spots.
📱
Installs Like a Native App
Add to home screen on any iPhone or Android — no app store needed. Works fast even with spotty job site signal.
How It Works
Up and running in an afternoon
No complicated setup. No IT department. If you can use a smartphone, you can run Partlocker.
1
Create your account
Sign up free, add your locations (vans, shop, warehouse), and invite your techs. They're in the moment you approve them.
2
Load your inventory
Add parts manually, scan receipts with your phone, or import from your HCP pricebook. Most shops are set up in an afternoon.
3
Let it track itself
Every completed HCP job queues deductions automatically. You review and approve. Stock stays accurate without anyone thinking about it.
