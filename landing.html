Features
Everything you need to
stay on top of your stock
📍
Per-Van Inventory
Every part is tracked by location — each tech's van, shop stock, or warehouse. See exactly what's where at a glance.
🔗
Housecall Pro Sync
Completed HCP jobs automatically queue inventory deductions. Review and approve with one tap — no double entry.
📷
Receipt Scanning
Snap a photo of any hardware store receipt and AI extracts the items and adds them to inventory automatically.
🤖
AI Part Matching
When a part name on an invoice doesn't match exactly, AI suggests the right inventory item based on price, date, and description.
⚠️
Low Stock Alerts
Set minimum quantities for critical parts. Get notified before you run out — not after a tech is stuck on a job.
📊
Usage Reports
See what's moving, who's pulling it, and what needs restocking. Full inventory, usage history, and reorder lists.
👥
Team Access Control
Techs can only pull from their own van. Admins see everything. Transfers are tracked. No more inventory blind spots.
📱
Mobile First
Install it from any phone — no app store needed. Works as a home screen app on iOS and Android, even with spotty signal.
How it works
Up and running in an afternoon
No complicated setup. No IT department required. If you can use a smartphone, you can run Partlocker.
1
Create your account
Sign up free, set up your locations (vans, shop, warehouse), and invite your team. They get access the moment you approve them.
2

3
Watch it track itself
Every completed HCP job queues deductions automatically. Techs log pulls from their van. You see everything in real time.
